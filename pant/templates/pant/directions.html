We are located in the Dairyville Village behind Alpenrose Dairy, which is in the Raleigh Hills neighborhood west of Portland. Enter at the lower gate at the field. If there is an event going on, you will have to park in the field and walk in. Otherwise, you can drive in and turn left around the Little League ball field and proceed up to the main driveway. Park in the small lot near the restrooms. Dairyville Village is through the gate to your right. We are in the corner behind the pavilion. The HO model railroad club is in the other corner.
